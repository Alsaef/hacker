_HACKER_

A hacker is a person who uses their technical skills and knowledge to gain unauthorized access to computer systems or networks. Not all hackers are criminals; some engage in hacking as a form of ethical or white hat hacking to identify and fix security vulnerabilities. However, black hat hackers use their skills to steal sensitive data, install malware, or cause other malicious activities. The term "hacker" has evolved over time and can refer to individuals or groups with varying degrees of expertise and motivations. As technology continues to advance, the role of hackers in identifying and mitigating security threats has become increasingly important.
